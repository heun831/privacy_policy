1. Scope

This Privacy Policy applies to the SpendWorld mobile application operated by hinhin (the “Application”). SpendWorld is a personal finance / budgeting app intended for adults. It is not directed to children and is not a Designed for Families product.

2. Information We Process

2.1 Information you enter

• Expense records (title/merchant, amount, category, date, notes, currency, installment information, necessity tags, card issuer labels).

• Budget and alarm settings (monthly budget, alert thresholds, customization text, end-of-day settings).

2.2 SMS / MMS / RCS (optional, on-device)

If you grant SMS-related permission and consent, the Application may read finance-related messages on your device to help detect card/bank transactions. Message bodies, sender addresses, and timestamps may be processed locally to suggest or register expenses. Pending message content may be stored on your device until you register, dismiss, or clear it. The Application does not upload your SMS inbox to SpendWorld servers.

2.3 Automatically collected technical data

• App diagnostics/crash logs via Firebase Crashlytics.

• Basic app usage events via Firebase Analytics (for example, that an expense was added or a budget alert fired).

• Advertising identifiers and ad-request signals when ads are shown (Google AdMob), unless you remove ads via in-app purchase.

• Standard network telemetry (such as IP address) processed by Google infrastructure when online features are used.

2.4 AI Spend Hearing (optional, when you ask)

The Application includes an optional AI Spend Hearing feature. When you submit a “buy or not?” question, the Application may send to Google Gemini (Google AI / generative language API):

• Your purchase intent (item name/description, price, optional notes);

• A short budget summary (monthly budget, remaining budget, recent daily pace);

• A limited recent expense summary (for example titles/merchants, amounts, categories, and dates — typically capped to recent month rows) so the model can compare similar spends.

This transmission happens only when you use the feature and request a verdict. SpendWorld does not operate its own AI servers; Google processes the request under Google’s terms and privacy policy. Hearing ask quotas and invite “water” balances are stored locally on your device.

2.5 Data we do not seek

We do not request contacts, precise location, microphone, or camera permission. We do not operate a social community feed, user-to-user debate board, or cloud chat inside the Application.

3. Where Data Is Stored

Your expense ledger, budgets, alarm settings, SMS consent state, and most preferences are stored locally on your device (Android DataStore). This is the primary copy of your financial records.

If Android Backup / device transfer is enabled, local Application data may be included in your device backup according to your OS settings.

When you use AI Spend Hearing, the prompt content described in section 2.4 is transmitted to Google for that request and is not stored by SpendWorld on a SpendWorld-operated server.

4. Third-Party Services

The Application uses third-party services that process data under their own privacy policies:

• Google Play Services / Google Privacy Policy

• Google Gemini API (AI Spend Hearing)

• Google AdMob

• Google Analytics for Firebase

• Firebase Crashlytics

• Google Play Billing (optional remove-ads / related purchases)

• Frankfurter FX API for currency conversion lookups (currency codes/dates only)

We may disclose information if required by law, to protect rights and safety, or to processors who work on our behalf under appropriate agreements.

5. Cookies and Tracking Technologies

The Application and its SDKs may use SDKs, advertising identifiers, and similar technologies for functionality, analytics, crash reporting, or advertising. Where required by law, consent will be obtained for non-essential tracking.

6. Purposes of Processing

• Provide budgeting, expense tracking, alerts, optional SMS-assisted entry, and optional AI Spend Hearing;

• Show ads or process remove-ads purchases;

• Measure stability and basic product usage;

• Comply with law.

7. Legal Bases (EEA/UK where applicable)

• Contract / service delivery: on-device budgeting features you request.

• Consent: SMS access, notifications/overlay where required, AI Spend Hearing requests (you choose to submit), and non-essential ads/analytics where consent is required.

• Legitimate interests: security and essential crash diagnostics, balanced against your rights.

8. International Transfers

Google and other providers may process data in the United States or other countries. Where required, transfers rely on appropriate safeguards such as Standard Contractual Clauses, adequacy decisions, or other lawful mechanisms, and/or your consent when legally needed.

9. Retention

• On-device user data: until you delete it in-app, clear app data, or uninstall (subject to device backups).

• Analytics / crash data: typically up to 24 months unless longer retention is required for security or legal compliance.

• Aggregated / anonymized data: may be retained longer because it no longer identifies you.

To request deletion of personal data we control, email heun831@gmail.com .

10. Your Rights

Depending on your location, you may have rights to access, correct, delete, restrict, or object to processing, and to withdraw consent. Contact heun831@gmail.com .

We will not discriminate against you for exercising these rights.

11. Children

The Application is not intended for children under 13 (or a higher age required in your country). We do not knowingly solicit data from children or market SpendWorld as a children’s product. If you believe a child has provided personal information, contact us so we can take appropriate steps.

12. Security

We use reasonable safeguards appropriate to a mobile app that stores financial records primarily on-device. No method of transmission or storage is 100% secure.

13. Data Breach Notification

If a breach affecting personal data occurs, we will provide notifications required by applicable law.

14. Opt-Out and Controls

• Limit ad tracking in Android settings; purchase remove-ads if offered.

2. 처리하는 정보

2.1 이용자가 입력하는 정보

• 지출 기록(상호/제목, 금액, 카테고리, 날짜, 메모, 통화, 할부, 필요성, 카드사 표기 등)

• 예산·알람 설정(월 예산, 경고 임계값, 알람 꾸미기 문구, 하루 마감 설정 등)

2.2 SMS/MMS/RCS (선택, 기기 내 처리)

이용자가 SMS 관련 권한과 동의를 부여한 경우에 한해, 카드·은행 거래 문자 등을 기기에서 읽어 지출 등록을 돕습니다. 메시지 본문·발신번호·수신시각 등은 기기 내 에서 처리·임시 보관될 수 있습니다. 전체 문자함 내용을 SpendWorld 자체 서버로 업로드하지 않습니다.

2.3 자동 수집되는 기술 정보

• Firebase Crashlytics를 통한 오류 진단 정보

• Firebase Analytics를 통한 기본 이용 이벤트(예: 지출 추가, 예산 알림 발생 여부)

• 광고 표시 시 AdMob을 통한 광고 ID 및 광고 요청 신호(광고 제거 구매 시 제한될 수 있음)

• 온라인 통신에 수반되는 IP 등 표준 네트워크 정보

2.4 AI 소비 청문회(선택, 이용자가 질문할 때)

본 앱에는 선택적 AI 소비 청문회 기능이 있습니다. “살까 말까?” 질문을 제출하면, 판결을 위해 다음 정보가 Google Gemini (Google AI / generative language API)로 전송될 수 있습니다.

• 구매 의도(품목명/설명, 가격, 선택적 메모)

• 예산 요약(월 예산, 잔여 예산, 최근 일별 페이스 등)

• 최근 지출 요약(상호/제목, 금액, 카테고리, 날짜 등 — 보통 당월 일부 행으로 제한)으로 유사 지출 비교

이 전송은 이용자가 해당 기능을 사용해 판결을 요청할 때만 이뤄집니다. SpendWorld는 자체 AI 서버를 운영하지 않으며, 요청은 Google의 약관·개인정보처리방침에 따라 처리됩니다. 청문회 질문 횟수·초대 “물” 잔액 등은 기기 내부에 저장됩니다.

2.5 수집하지 않는 정보

연락처·정확한 위치·마이크·카메라 권한을 요구하지 않습니다. 소셜 커뮤니티, 이용자 간 토론 게시판, 클라우드 채팅은 운영하지 않습니다.
